Kingdom Rule

Learn About the Rule

3076 & Kingdom Chat: Respect and no drama
  • Rise of Kingdoms is a game where we all want to have fun. Therefore, we ask you to remain courteous and avoid drama. Respect all players inside your alliance and kingdom. Avoid political or religious topics. These complex subjects can result in controversial and offensive posts. We have zero tolerance for any kind of hate speech, provocation, racism, sexism or otherwise offensive content.
  • Spotted mistreatment and/or disrespect of any individual will result in an immediate warning. Repeating such actions will ban you from Top 3 alliances.
  • If you have a problem with a player, leadership is available to help you resolve the conflict.

    Be happy !
Farm accounts
  • Beware that farm accounts are not allowed in Zone 3. Any accounts farm accounts found will be zeroed.
Gathering resources
  • Free farming in all zones through the Kingdom. But try to stick to your own territory.
  • Always opt to collect resources inside your alliance territory. You receive 25% gathering speed + that way you contribute 1% of collected resources to your alliance storehouse.
  • If you see a line going to a resource tile that means that tile is taken. Do not steal tiles.
  • Finish your tiles. By not finishing your resource tile you're limiting respawning of resources.
Attacking other players
  • Attacking cities is strictly forbidden! City hits must be authorized by an R5.
Guardians
  • Attacking guardians has its fixed time in each zone. It is expected from each player to respect the timeline and follow the etiquette of such event.
